Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

Syvaq ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, including:

  • Name and contact information
  • School and graduation year
  • Location data (with your consent)
  • Emergency contact information

2.2 Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information about your device and how you interact with our app, including:

  • Device information and identifiers
  • App usage patterns and preferences
  • Location data when using safety features

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our safety services
  • Send emergency alerts and notifications
  • Improve our app and user experience
  • Communicate with you about updates and features
  • Ensure the safety and security of our community

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except:

  • With emergency services when necessary
  • With your designated emergency contacts
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect the safety and rights of our users

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of certain communications
  • Control location sharing permissions
